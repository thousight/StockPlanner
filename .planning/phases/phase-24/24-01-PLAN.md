## Verification Criteria
- [ ] Every execution attempt is recorded in the audit log with the full payload and thread context.
- [ ] Concurrent execution requests beyond the limit are queued via the semaphore.
- [ ] PII detected in sandbox output is successfully redacted before reaching the agent.
- [ ] The system remains stable during a concurrency spike of 20+ simultaneous requests.

## must_haves
- Full payload logging is mandatory for forensic auditing.
- `sandbox.kill()` must be verified to trigger in all failure scenarios.
- The 5s timeout must be strictly enforced at the E2B level.

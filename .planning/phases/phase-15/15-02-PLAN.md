## Verification Criteria
- [ ] `ResearchAgent` successfully triggers SEC extraction for a given ticker.
- [ ] LLM-based delta detection correctly identifies material changes in a "Golden Pair" of filings.

- Delta detection must be "semantic" (LLM-driven), not just literal diffs.
- The Analyst must acknowledge SEC findings in its final valuation logic.
- The Summarizer must not lose SEC context when condensing the report.
